Financial intelligence, on your terms — and at your pace.

AI2FI is a coaching companion for the slow, durable work of building financial confidence — while learning to use AI. It's not a budgeting app. It's not a robo-advisor. It's a place to learn to think clearly about money — your money, your life, your timeline — with a coach that remembers what you've learned, notices what's changed, and grows with you over the years.

Most financial tools optimize for transactions. This one optimizes for understanding. Numbers matter, but the goal is the judgment behind the numbers: knowing why you're doing what you're doing, what you'd do differently if circumstances changed, and what to revisit next quarter.

How you'll use it

AI2FI lives across two surfaces. They're designed to do different things well — and to stay out of each other's way.

AI Co-Pilot — the place to think

This is where the coaching happens. Bring your favorite AI tool (Claude, ChatGPT, Grok, Gemini, ...). Have a conversation. You'll be asked questions. You'll work through scenarios, run knowledge checks, and journal what you're noticing. Your AI Co-Pilot will know your history, your goals, and what you've already covered, so each session picks up where the last one left off rather than starting from scratch.

A few of the things you can do here:

  • Quarterly check-ins — a lightweight assessment that surfaces what's changed and what needs attention.
  • Module deep-dives — work through one topic at a time (investing, retirement, mortgage strategy, charitable giving, and more) at the tier that fits your current comfort level.
  • Knowledge checks — short, conversational quizzes that meet you where you are and unlock the next tier when you're ready.
  • Open-ended questions — anything from "should I rebalance?" to "I just got a job offer with RSUs — how do I think about this?"

The tone here is warm and curious. There are no wrong answers, and nothing is graded. Comfort grows in stages: emerging → comfortable → confident → mastery.

The web dashboard — the place to see

This is where you watch your story unfold. The dashboard is an analysis and review tool: it visualizes what you've built up over time. You'll have modules to track your net worth, analyze your investments, and decide how best to tackle your debt.

What lives here:

  • Net worth trends over time, broken down the way you care about.
  • Goal tracking — where you said you wanted to be, where you actually are, and what's changed.
  • Portfolio — holdings, closed positions, tax-loss harvesting candidates, moat analysis on companies you own.
  • Module summaries — what you've covered, where you're confident, where you've flagged questions to revisit.
  • Session history — the journal of your conversations with Your Favorite AI Co-Pilot, in your own words.

The dashboard is honest, not flattering. No streaks. No badges. No dopamine loops. Just a clear picture of where you stand.

Where to start

  • If this is your first session: open your favorite AI Co-Pilot and run /financial-check-in. It will introduce itself, ask a handful of questions to understand your situation, and route you to the most useful next step. The conversation takes about fifteen minutes.
  • If you've been here before: pick up where you left off — your AI Co-Pilot will remember.
  • If you just want to look around: explore the dashboard. Nothing you do here will change your data.
