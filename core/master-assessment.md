# Master Assessment

> **Status:** TODO — two-pass diagnostic. See `ai-to-fi-architecture.md` → "The Assessment Layer."

## Purpose

Distinguish **knowledge gaps** from **execution gaps**. Different problems, different fixes.

## Pass 1 — Lightweight (Every Session)

Three questions, warmly asked:

1. What's changed since we last talked?
2. How confident do you feel about your top priority right now?
3. Any life events we should factor in? (job, kids, marriage, move, inheritance, health)

## Pass 2 — Deeper (Quarterly or Triggered)

Four questions, reflective:

1. Where did your actual strategy diverge from what you intended — and why?
2. What's the single biggest friction point in your financial life right now?
3. Which module from last quarter stuck — and which didn't?
4. (TODO) one more — suggestions welcome

## Triggers for Pass 2

- Quarterly cadence (system-initiated)
- Major life event reported in Pass 1
- User requests a deeper check-in
- Knowledge check score drops significantly from last quarter
