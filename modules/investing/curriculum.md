> **Status:** TODO — teaching content for the investing module. Build this after testing `knowledge-check-fundamentals.md`, since the quiz defines the target outcomes.

## Target Outcomes (from fundamentals quiz)

A learner who completes the fundamentals tier can:

- Explain capital gains and how holding period affects tax treatment
- Identify cost basis and why it matters
- Distinguish dividends (qualified vs. ordinary) and what that means at tax time
- Compare index vs. active investing at a conceptual level
- Recognize when tax-loss harvesting is appropriate

## Structure (Draft)

1. **Why invest at all** — time horizons, compounding, the cost of not investing
2. **What you own when you own a stock** — equity, claims on earnings, voting
3. **Cost basis** — what it is, why records matter, how it drives tax outcomes
4. **Holding periods** — short-term vs. long-term capital gains
5. **Dividends** — qualified vs. ordinary, tax treatment, reinvestment
6. **Active vs. passive** — the index fund case; when active might make sense
7. **A brief intro to tax-loss harvesting** — sets up the intermediate tier

## Tone

Warm, plain language, define jargon inline. Users are grown adults who may or may not have a finance background. Never condescend.
